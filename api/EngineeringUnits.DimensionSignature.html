The physical dimension of a quantity, expressed as a signed exponent for each SI base dimension plus a pseudo-dimension for plane angle (radians).

Remarks

Eight slots in total: length (L), mass (M), time (T), electric current (I), thermodynamic temperature (Θ), amount of substance (N), luminous intensity (J), and plane angle (A). The angle slot is a deliberate non-SI addition: it lets the library distinguish frequency (1/s) from angular velocity (rad/s), and linear power from rotational power. See specification §14.15.
