Remarks

Preferences are a per-dimension map (Decision 14.17). There is no binary SI-vs-US toggle because even within SI the conventionally-preferred unit depends on the dimension and the application — meter for length, kilogram for mass, but kilometer for travel and millimeter for machining.

Built-in profiles (SIScientific, SIEveryday, UsCustomary, UsEngineering, OilAndGas, Machining) cover common contexts. Clone() any of them to customize.
